How it works:
When the President travels, the FAA issues Temporary Flight Restrictions (TFRs) under FAR 91.141. These VIP TFRs typically have a 30 nautical mile diameter (15 NM radius) and are centered on the President's location. This app monitors FAA NOTAM data for these specific TFRs to determine the likely presidential location.
